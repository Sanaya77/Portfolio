My first publication on IoT-based electric motors: An incredible experience!

September 17, 2024

The Internet of Things (IoT) has significantly transformed various industries, and its integration with electric motors is paving the way for smarter, more efficient technologies. IoT-based electric motors, particularly in applications like **C-V2X** (Cellular Vehicle-to-Everything) and **V2V** (Vehicle-to-Vehicle), enable real-time communication and data exchange between vehicles, infrastructure, and other connected devices. These innovations allow electric motors in vehicles to operate more efficiently, with features like predictive maintenance, optimized energy consumption, and enhanced safety.
